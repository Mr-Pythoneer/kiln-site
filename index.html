01 — Private by default

Nothing you type ever leaves your computer.

Your AI runs on your own machine — so the things you tell it (your notes, your files, your customers, your half-finished ideas) never get sent to anyone. No company reading it. No data quietly reused to train something else. Privacy isn't a promise you have to take on faith; it's simply where the computer is.

02 — Yours to keep

Pay once. Keep it forever.

Your AI is something you own, not something you rent. Set it up once and it's yours — no monthly bill, no expiration, no company that can raise the price, change how it behaves, or switch it off. Ten years from now it still works exactly the way it did on day one.

03 — No subscription

No meter running. Nothing to cancel.

You're not paying by the month or by the message, and you're not logging in to somebody else's service. No surprise bill, no "your plan has changed" email. You paid for it, you have it, and that's the whole transaction — the way owning a thing used to work.

04 — Made for you

One AI, built to do your one thing.

Not a giant do-everything chatbot you have to wrestle into behaving — a small, focused helper made around the exact work you do over and over: your task, your style, your way. It shows up already knowing the job, so you're not typing a paragraph of instructions every single time.

05 — Works anywhere

Works even with the internet off.

Because it lives on your device, your AI keeps working on a plane, in a clinic, on a job site, or anywhere the wifi drops. No connection required, no outage on someone else's servers, no "service unavailable." It's always there — because it's yours.
